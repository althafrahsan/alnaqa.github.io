About AlNaqa Water

Established in 2010, AlNaqa Water has been providing premium quality mineral water to homes and businesses across the UAE. Our commitment to purity and excellence has made us a trusted name in the industry.

Our Story & Mission

AlNaqa Water was founded with a simple mission: to provide the purest mineral water while preserving the natural environment of our region. Our water is sourced from protected underground aquifers in Ajman, ensuring its natural mineral composition remains intact.

Eco-Friendly Practices

We use sustainable bottling processes and recyclable materials to minimize our environmental impact.

Rigorous Quality Control

Every batch of AlNaqa water undergoes extensive testing to ensure it meets the highest standards of purity.

Community Commitment

We support local initiatives and provide clean drinking water to communities in need across the UAE.

Our Quality Assurance

At AlNaqa Water, we maintain the highest standards of quality control to ensure every drop of water meets our rigorous purity standards.

Microbiological Testing

Every batch undergoes comprehensive microbiological analysis to ensure complete absence of harmful bacteria and pathogens.

Chemical Analysis

We test for over 50 chemical parameters to guarantee optimal mineral balance and absence of contaminants.

Certifications

Certified by UAE Ministry of Health, ESMA, and ISO 22000 for food safety management systems.

Our Advanced Purification Process

AlNaqa Water employs a multi-stage purification process that combines cutting-edge technology with natural filtration methods to deliver water of exceptional purity and taste.

1

Natural Filtration

Water passes through layers of volcanic rock and sand for natural purification.

2

Reverse Osmosis

Advanced membrane technology removes dissolved solids and impurities.

3

UV Treatment

Ultraviolet light eliminates any remaining microorganisms.

4

Mineral Enhancement

Essential minerals are carefully balanced for optimal taste and health benefits.
